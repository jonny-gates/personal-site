Rinkl

Rinkl has one simple goal. Allowing it's users to get in touch with their favourite social media influencers and recieve a personalised response. It's built in Ruby on Rails. It allows users to send each other video messages and influencers to recieve money for responding to Rinkls using Stripe's Connect API.

GapTools

GapTools is an online negotiation planning tool. GapTools takes advantage of IndexedDB to allow users to continue to work in the browser even without an internet connection.

AJA Domestic Services

AJA Domestic Services is a cleaning company based in Buckinghamshire. I built them a public facing website with a simple booking system which allowed them their clients to manage their bookings over a secure platform.
