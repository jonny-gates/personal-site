Snap Desk

Snap Desk encourages interaction in co-working space. Users can book desks and meeting rooms, arrange lunches, host events and tailor their working environment. Snap Desk introduces a virtual currency, Snap Bucks, which allow users to get the most out of their co-working space by being an active member of the community.

Snap Desk was created during Hack Coworking 2019 and was a HubHub Coworking category winner.

Rinkl

Rinkl has one simple goal. Allowing it's users to get in touch with their favourite social media influencers and recieve a personalised response. It's built in Ruby on Rails. It allows users to send each other video messages and influencers to recieve money for responding to Rinkls using Stripe's Connect API. Currently in closed beta - Rinkl will be released to the public soon.
